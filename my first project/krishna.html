Krishnapatnam Beach

Krishnapatnam is a significant port and center of the economy. The port now has 10 strong water berths.
Krishnapatnam port is one of the very few ports in the globe capable of handling 1,50,000 tons of gigantic vessels with load capacity. The port of Krishnapatnam is being upgraded to the largest private world-class port in India. The Navayuga Company is upgrading it. Iron ore and granite are exported to other nations such as China from this port.

Krishnapatnam – Port, Beach, Photos, Nellore, Andhra Pradesh Krishnapatnam Beach Nellore: Krishnapatnam beach is one of Nellore’s lovely beaches. It is located 24 km away from Nellore City in Nellore District, Andhra Pradesh, India’s southernmost coastal district, and 200 km south of Chennai.
