MYPADU BEACH

Mypadu Beach is located on the west coast of Bay of Bengal, at a distance of 25 km from Nellore in Andhra Pradesh. Mypadu beach is known for its golden sands and lush accommodation in luxurious Beach Cottages.
The crystal clear waters at Mypadu Beach make it wonderful for swimming, besides enjoying the natural scenic beauty of the beach, visitors are also enthralled with the water sports facilities at the beach side.

Mypadu Beach is located on the west coast of Bay of Bengal, at a distance of 25 km (16 mi) from Nellore in Andhra Pradesh. The beach is maintained by the state tourism board, APTDC. The beach provides fishing opportunities for the local fishermen, and access to cruises for the tourists.[1][2] The Andhra Pradesh Tourism Development Corporation (APTDC), is taking certain measures to promote Mypadu Beach as atourist destination by setting up recreational activities such as water sports and development of resorts.
