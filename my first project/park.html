CHIDRENS PARK

Children's park is one of the prominent parks in Nellore. The park has a vast web of the roadways. One can gain an easy access to the park by availing the service of the local transit like the buses and taxies to reach the place. It is located in Adithya Ramji Nagar, Nellore.


It is one of the beautifully maintained park with variety of flowers and plants. It is best place for children to enjoy and have fun. It consist of tall shady trees. The park houses water fountains and many slides for children. There is also a swimming pool, a skating ring and many swings within the park. The park is covered with lush green grass, with beautiful and seasonal flowers all around. There were benches for comfortable seating and swings at every corner for children to play. The park also was equipped with other playing items for the children like see - saw and other exciting games. There is a security guard always present near the pool for the safety of the children. The park had a wifi zone and a radio broadcast zone in it, which also pooled the gathering of youngsters and adults. The jogging stip is very popular among the locals.

Musical fountain present in the park also draw the attention of the crowd. The fountain adorns the beauty of the park. There are many food stalls present outside the park. The food stall setup here offer snack items.
It is recommended to visit the Children Park during timings 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM. The park remains open for all days of week. The entry fee for children is INR 50 for adults and INR 30 INR for children. It is a best place to spend time with your families specially to enjoy with your kids.
